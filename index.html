Cyber Safety 101

Learn how to protect yourself from phishing scams and weak passwords.

🚨 Phishing Awareness

Phishing is when attackers try to trick you into sharing sensitive information like passwords or credit card numbers.

  • Check the sender’s email address carefully.
  • Don’t click suspicious links or download attachments.
  • Hover over links to preview where they go.

Question: You receive an email from your “bank” asking to verify your account. What should you do?

🔒 Strong Password Tips

  • Use at least 12 characters (mix of letters, numbers, and symbols).
  • Never reuse passwords across sites.
  • Use a password manager for security and convenience.
